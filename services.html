How we work

The treatment path.

Most journeys at Dental Den follow these four steps. Some are quicker, some longer — but the rhythm is always the same.

— STEP 01

Consult

30-minute appointment with a full exam, digital X-rays, and an honest discussion. No commitment.

— STEP 02

Plan

A written treatment plan with phases, costs, insurance breakdown, and recommended timeline.

— STEP 03

Treat

The work itself — done in as few visits as possible, using sedation or laser where it improves the experience.

— STEP 04

Maintain

Follow-up checkups every six months to keep what we built in shape — and catch new issues early.
