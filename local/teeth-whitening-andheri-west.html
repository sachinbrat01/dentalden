What is Teeth Whitening?

Over time, lifestyle habits such as drinking tea, coffee, or smoking can leave deep organic stains on your dental enamel, dulling your smile. Frequently asked questions about teeth-whitening in Andheri West

Does professional teeth whitening damage enamel?

No. Under professional supervision, our whitening gels only target organic stain molecules inside the enamel pores. They do not alter the physical structure of your enamel or weaken your teeth.

How long do the whitening results last?

Results typically last 1 to 2 years, depending on your diet. Avoiding heavy staining foods, brushing regularly, and using our home touch-up kit will extend your bright smile significantly.
