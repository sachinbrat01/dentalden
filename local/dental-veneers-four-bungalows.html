What is Dental Veneers?

Dental veneers are ultra-thin, custom-fabricated shells of E-Max ceramic or composite resin that are permanently bonded to the front of your teeth. For patients in Four Bungalows looking to correct deep internal discolouration, spacing, chipped edges, or minor crowding, veneers offer a rapid, life-changing cosmetic solution.

Frequently asked questions about dental-veneers in Four Bungalows

What is the difference between porcelain and composite veneers?

Porcelain veneers (E-Max) are custom-made in a laboratory, highly durable (15+ years), completely stain-resistant, and mimic natural tooth translucency perfectly. Composite veneers are hand-layered directly on your teeth in a single visit, are more cost-effective, but have a shorter lifespan (5-7 years) and can stain over time.

Is the veneer procedure reversible?

Porcelain veneers require a very micro-layer of enamel removal (about 0.3mm), meaning the tooth will always need to be covered. Direct composite bonding can sometimes be performed with zero preparation, making it technically reversible.
