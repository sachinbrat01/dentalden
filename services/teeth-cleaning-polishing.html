About this service

Why choose Dental Den
for Polishing?

Regular professional cleaning is the single most important thing for dental health. Our sessions include ultrasonic scaling to remove tartar, followed by polishing to smooth surfaces and remove stains. We perform a comprehensive exam during each visit. Recommended every six months. Dental Den serves families across Andheri West, Lokhandwala, Oshiwara, and Kandivali.
