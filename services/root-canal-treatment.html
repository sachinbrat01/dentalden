About this service

Why choose Dental Den
for Treatment?

Root canal treatment saves teeth that would otherwise need extraction. We use modern rotary endodontic instruments and apex locators for precise treatment, often completed in a single sitting. Dr. Tanya's gentle technique means most patients experience zero pain. We seal the tooth with premium materials and recommend a protective crown. Trusted by patients across Andheri West, Lokhandwala, Oshiwara, and Kandivali.
