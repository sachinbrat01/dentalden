About this service

Why choose Dental Den
for Reconstruction?

Full mouth reconstruction is a comprehensive treatment plan for patients whose teeth, gums, and bite have been severely compromised by decay, trauma, or neglect. Dr. Tanya coordinates implants, crowns, bridges, orthodontics, and periodontal therapy into a single staged plan. Every case begins with digital scans, bite analysis, and photographs. We serve complex cases from across Mumbai, including Andheri West, Lokhandwala, Oshiwara, and Kandivali neighborhoods.

Common questions

How long does it take?

Typically 3-12 months depending on complexity. We stage treatment to be manageable.

Is it expensive?

We provide phased plans with EMI options. A written estimate is provided before any work begins.

Can I eat during treatment?

Yes - we use temporary restorations between stages.
