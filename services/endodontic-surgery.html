About this service

Why choose Dental Den
for Surgery?

When a root canal-treated tooth does not heal, endodontic surgery may save it. Apicoectomy involves removing the root tip and sealing the canal from below. Dr. Tanya also performs re-treatments for complex cases. These microsurgical techniques preserve teeth that might otherwise need extraction. Available to patients from Andheri West, Lokhandwala, Oshiwara, and Kandivali.

Common questions

Frequently asked.

When is it needed?

When a root canal-treated tooth shows persistent infection.

What is the success rate?

85-95% when performed by trained specialists.

How long is recovery?

Mild swelling for 2-3 days. Full healing in 1-2 weeks.
