About this service

Why choose Dental Den
for Dentistry?

A smile makeover at Dental Den is never one-size-fits-all. Dr. Tanya designs every transformation around your facial proportions, lip dynamics, and personal aesthetic goals. Using digital smile design software, you preview your new smile in 3D before any work begins. Treatments may include porcelain veneers, composite bonding, gum contouring, or teeth whitening. Serving patients from Andheri West, Lokhandwala, Oshiwara, and Kandivali, we believe cosmetic dentistry should enhance who you already are.
