Common questions

Frequently asked.

What causes TMJ disorders?

Teeth grinding, jaw clenching, stress, arthritis, jaw injury, or bite misalignment.

Do I need surgery?

Most TMJ disorders respond to conservative treatment.

How long does treatment take?

Many patients improve within 4-8 weeks.
