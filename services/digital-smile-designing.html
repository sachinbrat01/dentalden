About this service

Why choose Dental Den
for Designing?

Digital Smile Design lets you see your new smile before any treatment starts. Using photographs, videos, and digital scans, Dr. Tanya creates a precise 3D simulation accounting for facial proportions, lip line, and gum architecture. You review, refine, and approve the design before committing. Available to patients across Andheri West, Lokhandwala, Oshiwara, and Kandivali.
